Setup guide

From sign-up to
first client in 30 minutes.

No technical knowledge needed. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use Espresso.

1

Create your Espresso account

Go to espresso.insure/trial, enter your name, email, and mobile number. Choose your plan — or start with the 14-day free trial. No credit card needed.

2

Save Maya's number

After sign-up, you'll receive Maya's WhatsApp number in your dashboard. Save it in your phone contacts as Maya — Espresso. This is the number you will add to client groups.

3

Upload your existing policies

In your dashboard, go to Clients → Import and upload a CSV of your current client policies. Use our template or let the importer map your existing columns. Maya immediately begins tracking all renewals.

4

Create your first client group

Open WhatsApp. Create a new group with yourself and a client. Add Maya's number to the group. That's it — Maya will introduce herself and take over the conversation.

5

Watch your dashboard fill up

Every client brief, every renewal flag, and every alert appears in your dashboard in real time. Log in once a day to stay on top of your entire book.

6

Control Maya from the group

Type Maya pause to silence her while you speak directly to the client. Type Maya take over to hand back control. Type Maya brief me for an instant client summary sent privately to you.
